about

Hello! I am Nadia Putri, web designer and developer, and programmer-in-training. I am passionate about design and programming, creativity and logic. I have always loved solving puzzles. I believe in the design philosophy: less is more.

Born and raised in Indonesia. I have lived in Australia since 2010. I have always had an interest in art and design, and started to take an interest in it by taking Graphic Design class when I was at high school, on top of experimenting with design on my own. I undertook work experience at the following places: UWA and Monk Design.

I also had a great interest in computers since I was a child, having taken computer lessons since I was 5. I currently have a partially completed Certificate III in IT from TAFE, on top of my Bachelor of Arts (Digital Design) from Curtin University, where I focused on web design and front-end web development. I am currently studying Master of IT at UWA, in order to further my knowledge of programming.
